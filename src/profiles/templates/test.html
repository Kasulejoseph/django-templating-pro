OUR VISION

We purpose to have associate where OVC live a quality life that quarantees their rights to realise full potential.

OUR MISSION

To promote the wellbeing of the OVC through coordinated service delivery that promotes OVC/Child rights for sustainable development.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • To strengthen the capacity of families, care givers, orphans & other service provider to protect and care for orphans and other vulnerable children.
  • To expand the provision of essential services of orphans and other vulnerable children and their household.
  • To increase access, to protect child and legal services for orphans and other vulneranable children
  • To strengthen institutional policy legal and other mechanism that provide supportive environment for a coordinated OVC response.
